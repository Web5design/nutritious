Nutritious

Browse, search, and tag textual content.

  • Search with keywords or regular expressions.
  • Build your own collection of topical tags of quotations in text.
  • Standard browsing and navigation.

Tips

  • Visit the Library to see resources available for browsing.
    • Currently resources are limited to a fixed, shared set.
    • In the future users might upload and share their own.
  • Search over textual content currently being viewed (e.g. current book or chapter).
    • Use keywords or patterns: "Adam.*ate".
    • Search for tags with the hash sign: "#funny"
    • Jump to the search bar with Ctrl-S.
  • Copy selected text from books with Ctrl-C.
  • Create tags by highlighting text and pressing Ctrl-T.
    • View tags in the "guest" account, or contact me to request an account to create your own tag collection.
    • Bulk import or export entire sets of tags.
